## APPENDIX

#### Airport Transfer Information

The Vienna Airport Lines take you quickly, directly and comfortably from Vienna Airport to the city centre or the Danube City and back.

Three express lines pick you up daily from various Viennese districts. Our buses stop at 11 stations with underground connections. Also take advantage of our night services on Vienna Airport Line 2 (Morzinplatz/ Schwedenplatz).

- **VAL 1:** [Line VAL 1](https://www.viennaairportlines.at/en/dam/jcr:04733850-c7b2-42f8-b22d-0b41908ce56c/fahrplan-val1-03072023.pdf) line runs around the clock from Vienna West Station via Vienna Central Station (South Entrance) to Vienna Airport. The intervals are 30 minutes, except between 00:30 a.m. and 02:30 a.m. Between these times there is only one bus at 01:30 a.m.
- **VAL 2:** [Line VAL 2](https://www.viennaairportlines.at/en/dam/jcr:5d011ed6-5969-4740-9a31-fad7353a255a/val2_02102023.pdf) line runs from Morzinplatz/ Schwedenplatz to Vienna Airport every half hour from 03:30 a.m. to 11:30 p.m.
- **VAL 3:** [Line VAL 3](https://www.viennaairportlines.at/en/dam/jcr:6cd82e33-f107-4471-8b32-9d1b5f2e3fdf/fahrplan-val3-01032024.pdf) line connects Donaustadt with Vienna Airport. The Vienna Airport Lines stop at the stations: Donauzentrum - Kagraner Brücke - Hotel NH Danube City - Kaisermühlen/VIC (Austria Center) - Krieau (Messe Wien) - Stadion - Donaumarina - Vienna Airport. Line VAL 3 runs every hour between 04:58 a.m. and 07:58 p.m. between Donauzentrum and Vienna Airport.
